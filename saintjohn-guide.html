🌄 Weather

Saint John is known for its dramatic weather -- fog, wind, and sudden changes are part of the charm. Summers are mild (18-25C), winters can be cold and snowy. Always worth checking before heading out.

🌳 Explore Saint John

Saint John is Canada's oldest incorporated city, perched on the Bay of Fundy -- home to the world's highest tides. There is a lot to discover.

Discover
Things To Do
Top-rated attractions and experiences rated by visitors
Must-See
Reversing Falls
The famous tidal phenomenon where the Saint John River reverses direction -- a unique natural wonder
Downtown
Market Square & Waterfront
Historic waterfront with shops, restaurants, and the Saint John City Market -- Canada's oldest farmers market
Near You
Bayshore Beach
A short walk from the property -- great spot to enjoy the Bay of Fundy
Nature
Irving Nature Park
600 acres of coastal trails, forests, and stunning Bay of Fundy views -- free to visit
What's On
Events This Week
Festivals, markets, concerts and local happenings in Saint John
Tip: The Bay of Fundy has the highest tides in the world -- up to 16 metres. Tide timing changes daily so check a tide chart if you plan to visit the beaches or Reversing Falls.
