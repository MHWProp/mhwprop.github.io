☺Welcome

We are so glad to have you here! This guide covers everything you need to feel right at home. If anything comes up during your stay, do not hesitate to reach out -- we are happy to help.

1.Unit Access
  • Once you know your arrival time, please let us know so we can arrange access for you.
  • For security purposes, access is strictly granted to the person who made the booking.
  • Please have a valid ID ready at check-in -- it must match the name on the reservation. No exceptions.
2.Parking
  • You are welcome to use the driveway -- space for up to 2 vehicles.
  • Please park on the left side near the lawn.
  • If two cars, park them in tandem -- one behind the other.
  • Street parking is also available during the day.
3.Patio Lights
  • The switch for the patio lights is located near the shed door.
4.House Guidelines

A few friendly reminders to keep things comfortable for everyone.

  • No pets or visiting animals, please.
  • Smoking inside the house is not allowed.
  • Please remove shoes indoors, especially on carpets and rugs.
  • The space is set up for the number of guests on your reservation -- overnight visitors are not recommended.
  • Quiet hours: 10 PM to 8 AM.
  • Please enjoy meals in the dining area to keep the bedrooms clean.
  • The basement and backyard shed are not accessible during your stay.
  • Please do not tamper with fire alarms, temperature sensors, or the modem.
  • Linens and furnishings are provided with care -- please treat them gently to avoid extra charges.
  • Towels and bathroom supplies are in the hallway closet.
If anything needs attention during your stay, just reach out -- we are always happy to help!
5.Thermostat
  • The Nest thermostat is located behind the TV in the living room.
  • Adjust the temperature by rotating the outer ring.
  • Keep floor vents clear for even air circulation.
  • Please use the thermostat rather than opening windows or doors to control the temperature.
6.TV
  • Enjoy Netflix, Prime Video, and Roku!
  • Before heading out, please leave the remote on the TV stand.
7.Washroom
Important -- Please Read

Do not flush toilet paper or any other items -- this includes tissues, paper towels, pads, wipes, flushable wipes, Q-tips, or tampons.

Please dispose of all these items in the garbage bin. Make sure there is a liner in the bin for easier disposal.

If the toilet becomes clogged, do not keep flushing -- it may overflow and cause damage. No water should ever reach the floor. If it does, dry it immediately and contact us right away.

Any clogging or overflow caused by improper use will be the responsibility of the guest, including the cost of any repairs.
8.Garbage and Recycling
  • Bins are located on the side of the house.
  • Blue bin for recycling -- black bin for garbage.
  • Garbage and recycling collection is managed on a regular schedule -- no action needed from you.
9.Laundry
  • There are no on-site laundry facilities available to guests.
  • A laundromat is just an 8-minute walk away at 700 Balmoral Dr.
10.Nearest Grocery
  • No Frills is located at 700 Balmoral Dr. -- a short walk away.
11.Check-Out Reminder

Before you leave, please take a moment to:

  1. Turn off all lights and electronics.
  2. Lock doors and securely close all windows.
  3. Clean up any dishes.
  4. Dispose of all garbage and remove any perishable food items.
Messages sent after midnight will be answered promptly in the morning.

We hope you have a fantastic stay!
